# PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated:** 19 November 2025

## 1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the privacy policy of Nama Economics Limited in respect of its trading name Masri Strategy. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website (masristrategy.com) or purchase our coaching services.

## 2. CONTROLLER

Nama Economics Limited is the controller and responsible for your personal data.

## 3. THE DATA WE COLLECT

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

- **Identity Data:** First name, last name, and video recordings of coaching sessions.
- **Contact Data:** Email address and telephone number.
- **Career & Education Data:** CVs, cover letters, academic transcripts, writing samples, career history, and notes taken during coaching calls.
- **Transaction Data:** Details about payments to and from you and details of services you have purchased. (Note: We do not store financial credit card data; this is handled by third-party processors).

## 4. HOW WE USE YOUR PERSONAL DATA

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

- **Performance of Contract:** To schedule calls, review your CV and job application drafts, and deliver the coaching services you purchased.
- **Legitimate Interests:** To send you our free guide ("Asymmetric Information") or reply to your inquiries.
- **Consent:** Where you have opted in to receive marketing communications via email.

## 5. USE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE (AI)

### 5.1 How we use AI
We use Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools to assist in the delivery of our Services. Specifically, we may upload your documents (such as your CV, cover letter, or writing samples) into secure, enterprise-grade AI environments to generate feedback, suggest edits, and simulate interview questions.

### 5.2 Data Security
We use reputable third-party AI providers (such as Google Gemini) who are subject to confidentiality obligations. We do not use AI to make automated legal or significant decisions about you (e.g., we do not use AI to reject your application to us—all decisions are made by human coaches).

## 6. DISCLOSURES OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

We may share your personal data with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our business. We do not sell your data. We share data with the following categories of service providers:

- **Payment Processors:** Service providers who process payments on our behalf (such as Stripe, Wise, and banking institutions).
- **Communication & IT Services:** Providers of email, video conferencing, calendar scheduling, and document hosting services (such as Google Workspace, Calendly, and Loom).
- **AI Service Providers:** Providers of Artificial Intelligence tools used to analyze documents and generate feedback (such as Google Gemini).
- **Marketing & Review Platforms:** Platforms used to collect client feedback and manage email communications (such as Trustpilot and email marketing software).

## 7. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Many of our external service providers (including Google, Stripe, and Calendly) are based outside the United Kingdom, particularly in the United States. This means your personal data may be transferred and stored internationally.

To protect your data, we only work with major, reputable service providers who adhere to strict data protection standards. We rely on the following legal frameworks to ensure your data is safe:

- **The UK-US Data Bridge:** Where providers are certified under the UK Extension to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.
- **Standard Contractual Clauses:** Where providers have implemented standard data protection clauses recognized by the UK government in their service agreements.

## 8. DATA SECURITY AND RETENTION

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost or accessed in an unauthorized way. We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

## 9. YOUR LEGAL RIGHTS

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or erase your personal data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at muath@masristrategy.com.
